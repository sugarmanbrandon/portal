Frequently asked questions

How does ampCX work?

AmpCX employs AI algorithms and natural language processing to analyze various forms of customer communication, such as phone calls, chats, and emails. After understanding customer queries in real-time, the system searches a dynamic knowledge base for relevant information, and then our proprietary GenAI technology interprets the results, generating accurate and personalized answers for CSRs to enhance the overall support experience.

How long does it take to set up?

The setup time for AmpCX can vary depending on the size and complexity of your business, as well as the volume of support materials to be ingested. Typically, the process takes between 4 to 6 weeks, which includes data integration, system customization, CSR training, and initial performance monitoring to ensure a smooth and effective implementation.

How will this improve my CX throughput?

AmpCX enhances your CX throughput by leveraging AI algorithms and GenAI technology to provide real-time, accurate information to customer service representatives, leading to faster response times and increased efficiency. This improvement enables your support team to handle more queries without compromising quality. Consequently, you'll experience higher customer satisfaction, increased retention, a more positive brand perception, and the ability to scale your business effectively while maintaining exceptional support standards.

What does onboarding look like?

Onboarding for AmpCX begins with data integration, where we work closely with your team to ingest support materials and create a comprehensive knowledge base. Next, we customize the AI algorithms and GenAI technology to align with your business needs, followed by tailored training sessions for customer service representatives. Our dedicated support team continuously monitors performance, ensuring optimal response accuracy and customer satisfaction throughout implementation and beyond.

How do you ensure data privacy?

AmpCX is committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data. We adhere to strict data protection policies and industry best practices, including GDPR and other relevant regulations. All data ingested and processed by our platform is encrypted both in transit and at rest. Access to sensitive information is restricted to authorized personnel only, and we conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to maintain the highest levels of data privacy. Furthermore, AmpCX can be customized to comply with any specific privacy requirements your business may have, ensuring a secure and compliant solution for your customer support needs.

Do you have a developer API?

Currently, AmpCX does not offer a developer API. Our focus is on providing a seamless and comprehensive solution for businesses, ensuring that the platform integrates efficiently with your existing customer support infrastructure and processes without requiring additional development work. However, we continually evaluate customer needs and industry trends, and the possibility of developing an API in the future is not ruled out based on customer demand and evolving requirements.
